Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats. Next time you go out to breakfast, try ordering an omelette instead of your usual fried eggs!

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 6 minutes
  • Prep Time: Approximately 2 minutes
  • Cook Time: Approximately 4 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp (15 mL) water
  • Pinch salt
  • Pinch pepper
  • 1 tbsp (15 mL) butter or oil
  • Filling ingredients: cheese, diced vegetables, or cooked meats

Instructions

Step 1

Whisk eggs, water, salt, and pepper.

Step 2

Melt butter in an 8-inch (20 cm) nonstick frying pan over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture. As eggs set around edge of pan, with spatula, gently push cooked portions toward centre of skillet. Tilt and rotate skillet to allow uncooked egg to flow into empty spaces.

Step 3

When eggs are almost set on surface but still look moist, cover half of omelette with filling. Slip spatula under unfilled side; fold over onto filled half.

Step 4

Cook for a minute, then slide omelette onto plate.
